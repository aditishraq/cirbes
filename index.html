The Problem

Beyond the linear take-make-dispose model

Environmental sustainability is often seen as a cost by businesses. But for businesses to be truly profitable, environmental considerations must be embedded in strategies for economic sustainability.

The solution lies in the Circular Economy — a regenerative approach where economic opportunities and environmental sustainability work hand in hand.

Learn About Circular Business →

Implementing Circular Economy principles requires a systematic assessment of all enterprise activities — business models, product design, manufacturing, supply chain, end-of-life management, and customer needs.
