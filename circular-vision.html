The Big Picture

What is Circular Business?

Businesses need growth sustainable from both economic and environmental perspectives. The solution lies in the Circular Economy.

The Problem

The linear model is running out of road

The bottom line of businesses is being affected by limiting access to resources. The rise of the middle class due to emerging economies puts unprecedented burden on natural resources, contributing to high market volatility.

This scenario requires a proactive approach to developing products and services with economic and environmental performance embedded in the business models.

Implementing Circular Economy principles in businesses requires a systematic assessment of all enterprise activities â€” including business models, product design, manufacturing, supply chain, end-of-life product management and customer needs.
