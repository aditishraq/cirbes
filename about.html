Our Story

CirBES Story

We are passionate about a sustainable future ‚Äî harmony between economic prosperity and our natural environment.

What we do

Enabling the circular transition

In our quest for alternative business models for sustained economic competitiveness of manufacturing industry ‚Äî in the wake of dwindling resources and deteriorating environment ‚Äî we started exploring the idea of resource reuse and its potential advantages.

We soon realised that the notion of circular systems has a potential to revolutionise the manufacturing industry. It stands for an entirely new way of 'doing business'. The idea of a start-up came during one of our intensively engaging discussions. Hence, CirBES was born!

With an engineering outlook, we proceed systematically ‚Äî analysing each individual company in a system perspective covering business model, product design, supply chain and IT infrastructure ‚Äî to provide expert advice and support in enabling a smooth transition from linear to circular systems.

What we aim for

CirBES operates as a 'change agent' supporting manufacturing industry to facilitate transitions from linear to circular systems. Our ambition is to provide end-to-end services in understanding needs, analysing potential and implementing solutions in going and growing circular.

Where we come from

We are engineering professionals from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm with backgrounds in manufacturing and sustainability. Research is both our profession and passion that has motivated us to rethink the role of manufacturing industry in socio-economic sustainability.
