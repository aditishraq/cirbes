CirBES Services

Apart from the services listed on this page the CirBES team gladly provides knowledge for circular business implementation on a case-to-case basis. Get in touch with us!

Awareness & knowledge building

Executive workshop:

In an interactive one-day workshop you can pick up fundamental concepts, drivers and opportunities of circular businesses. With this know-how you will be able to identify your strengths, weaknesses and necessary future steps for growing circular.

Training on circular manufacturing operations:

In addition to fundamental concepts, drivers and opportunities our interactive workshop will provide you with an in-depth understanding of circular business operations. Based on the philosophy "I Hear and I Forget, I See and I Remember, I Do and I Understand" the CirBES team has developed a board game to provide a hands-on learning experience in a multi-disciplinary setting. By experiencing different business scenarios and decision points you will be able to envisage challenges and opportunities of transitioning from linear to circular businesses.

Assessment & potential analysis

Circularity quick-check:

In case you are curious about your readiness to go circular CirBES offers a quick assessment based on standardized procedures. This quick assessment provides sufficient depth to identify hot spots for further analysis.

Circularity in-depth analysis:

CirBES has its roots in research, development and application of complex models to identify potentials of circular product systems. The CirBES team can support you with the following advanced analyses to gain explanatory insights into your circular product system potential:

  • Exploration of circular business opportunities (download PDF)
  • Optimization of end-of-life design strategies (download PDF)
  • Prediction of market demands during business model changes (download PDF)

Implementation support

End-to-end or advisory support:

To go circular CirBES can either coordinate entire implementation projects or take an advisory role. In doing so, CirBES uses state-of-the-art methodologies and tools including those developed by the CirBES team. All activities are managed to maximize value recovery both from economic and environmental perspectives.
